Profile

Hi there! My name is Calvin and I am an aspiring software engineer. I thoroughly enjoy learning new things and meeting cool people. I find everything interesting, but I've miraculously been able to narrow down my interests to all that makes me smile. I smile at everything.

Right now, I am studying Computer Science at UCLA. On campus, I am part of the Daily Bruin's online team, where I work with other cool people to make awesome web features and packages. I am also interning at Yahoo!, where I am working with the Sports team to provide users with a better frontend experience.

In my free time, I carry out my duties as a professional connoisseur of life. I enjoy traveling and exploring new places because I find it fascinating to observe how people from different cultures interact with each other. I love tinkering away on a piano or guitar, and find no guilt "wasting" time to discover new artists. I am an avid artist, photographer, and videographer because I love being able to capture my perspective of life through different mediums.

I started this website as means of showcasing my work in web design/development. But it has turned out to be so much more than that. It has become a timeline of my personal development through various projects and jobs. It shows where I started, and how far I have come. It has become something I am proud of.
